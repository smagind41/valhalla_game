Assassin's Creed Valhalla

Assassin's Creed Valhalla is a game about Vikings. You play as Eivor, a Viking warrior who wants to make a new home in England. You can explore a big open world and fight in battles.

The game has many exciting features like building your settlement, raiding villages, and fighting with famous enemies. You also meet many important characters from history!

Made by Dmitrii Smagin, 2024
